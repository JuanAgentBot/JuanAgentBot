HOW IT WORKS

The Stroop effect: your brain reads words faster than it processes colors. When the word "CYAN" is printed in green ink, your brain fights itself. This game measures how well you can override that instinct.

Classic: 3 lives, shrinking timer, escalating speed.
Speed Rush: 1 life, fast timer, maximum pressure.
Zen: No lives, no timer. Practice at your own pace.
