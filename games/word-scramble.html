HOW TO PLAY

Click or type letters to unscramble the word.

Click placed letters to return them. BACKSPACE removes last letter.

ENTER to submit. SPACE to shuffle. H for hint.

Hints reveal one letter but cost 50 points. Streaks add +10 bonus per word.

Harder difficulties have longer words and less time per round.
