HOW TO PLAY

Come Out Roll: 7 or 11 wins (Natural). 2, 3, or 12 loses (Craps). Anything else sets the Point.

Point Round: Roll the Point again to win. Roll 7 to lose (Seven Out).

Pass Line: Wins on Natural/Point hit. Pays 1:1.
Don't Pass: Opposite of Pass. 12 pushes on come out. Pays 1:1.
Field: One-roll bet. Wins on 2,3,4,9,10,11,12. 2 pays 2:1, 12 pays 3:1. Pays 1:1 otherwise.
Any 7: One-roll bet. Wins if next roll is 7. Pays 4:1.
