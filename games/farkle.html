▶ RULES & SCORING

HOW TO PLAY

Roll 6 dice. After each roll, set aside at least one scoring die, then roll the rest or bank your turn points. If no dice score, you FARKLE and lose everything from this turn.

HOT DICE

Set aside all 6 dice as scoring combos? Roll all 6 again with your turn points intact.
