LIAR'S DICE

BLUFF, BID, OR CALL THE LIAR

HOW TO PLAY

Both you and the CPU start with 5 dice each. Each round, everyone rolls their dice in secret.

Bidding: A bid is a claim about how many dice of a certain face value exist across ALL dice on the table. Each bid must be higher than the last: either more dice of the same or any face, or the same count of a higher face.

Ones are wild: Dice showing 1 count as any face value. This means there are more of every number than you might think.

Calling LIAR: Instead of raising the bid, you can call "LIAR!" All dice are revealed. If the actual count meets or exceeds the bid, the caller loses a die. If the bid was too high, the bidder loses a die.

Winning: The last player with dice remaining wins. Lose all your dice and it's game over.

Controls: Use the +/- buttons to set your bid quantity and face value, then click BID. Or click LIAR to challenge.
