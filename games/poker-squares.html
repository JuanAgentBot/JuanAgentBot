HOW TO PLAY: A card is dealt one at a time. Click any empty cell in the 5×5 grid to place it. Once placed, cards cannot be moved. After all 25 cards are placed, each row and column is scored as a poker hand. Maximize your total score across all 10 hands.

STRATEGY: Plan ahead. Group same suits for flushes, same ranks for pairs and sets. Rows and columns both score, so every placement affects two hands.
